Операции над объектами в конвейере

	Sort-Object
		-Property
		-Descending

	Select-Object
		-Property
		@{n="..."; e={...}}
		-First
		-Last
		-Skip

	Where-Object
		-EQ, -NE, -GT, -Like...
		-FilterScript {...}

	Measure-Object
		-Property
		-Sum, -Minimum, Maximum, Average

	Group-Object
		-Property

	ForEach-Object
		-MemberName
		-Process {...}
